About My Work

With expertise in hardware-software co-design, I focus on developing reliable PCBs and firmware for biomedical implants. My projects emphasize biocompatibility, miniaturization, signal integrity, and compliance with standards like ISO 13485, ensuring safe and efficient medical devices.

Key Focus Areas

  • PCB Design: Flexible and rigid-flex boards for implants, prioritizing thermal stability and EMI shielding.
  • Firmware Development: Embedded systems for data processing, power management, and real-time control in medical hardware.
  • Daily Projects: Documenting advancements in silicon validation, RF integration, and scripting for biomedical applications.
